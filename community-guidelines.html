# VIBEKISS COMMUNITY GUIDELINES ## ⚠️ Safety Reminder Your safety matters. While VIBEKISS provides reporting tools, moderation systems, and community guidelines designed to help create a safer environment, users must always exercise personal judgment and caution when interacting with others on the platform. **For your safety, please remember to:** * Protect your personal information. * Be cautious when sharing contact details. * Meet new people in public places. * Inform friends or family if you plan to meet someone in person. * Arrange your own transportation when meeting someone. * Report suspicious or inappropriate behavior through the app. > **If something feels wrong, trust your instincts and discontinue the interaction immediately.** --- ## Introduction VIBEKISS is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and authentic environment where adults can connect and build meaningful relationships. These Community Guidelines establish the standards of behavior expected from all users of the VIBEKISS platform. By accessing or using VIBEKISS, you agree to comply with these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, content removal, feature restrictions, account suspension, or permanent account termination. --- ## 1. Age Requirement VIBEKISS is strictly intended for adults. * Users must be at least **18 years old**, or the age of legal majority in their jurisdiction (whichever is higher), to create an account or use the platform. * Accounts suspected of belonging to individuals under the age of 18 may be removed immediately. * VIBEKISS may report suspected violations involving minors to relevant authorities where required by law. ## 2. Respectful Conduct Users must treat others with respect and dignity. The following behavior is strictly prohibited: * Harassment or bullying. * Threats or intimidation. * Abusive or degrading language. * Encouraging harmful or dangerous behavior. * Repeated unwanted contact after someone declines interaction. ## 3. Harassment and Abuse Harassment, intimidation, or abusive behavior toward other users is prohibited. This includes persistent unwanted messages, insults, emotional coercion, or pressure to share personal photos and sensitive information. ## 4. Hate Speech and Discrimination VIBEKISS maintains a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech. Users may not post content targeting individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, nationality, gender identity, sexual orientation, or disability. ## 5. Authentic Profiles Users must represent themselves truthfully. Prohibited actions include: * Creating fake profiles or impersonating another individual. * Using images of celebrities or others without permission. * Providing misleading identity information. ## 6. Profile Content Standards Profile photos and user content must comply with community standards. Prohibited content includes graphic violence, illegal activities, and hate symbols. ## 7. Sexual Content and Solicitation VIBEKISS is a dating platform but is not intended for explicit sexual content. * Users may not share pornographic material or offer sexual services. * Content involving minors in any sexual context will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement. ## 8. Manipulation and Coercive Behavior Users may not manipulate, pressure, or exploit others. This includes fabricated emergencies, threatening reputational harm, or exploiting vulnerable individuals. ## 9. Scams and Financial Exploitation Users may not use the platform for financial exploitation. Prohibited activities include requesting money, gift cards, cryptocurrency, or promoting investment solicitations. ## 10. Spam and Promotional Activity Mass messaging, promotional links, marketing campaigns, and collecting user data for external databases are prohibited. ## 11. Privacy and Personal Information Users must respect the privacy of others. Prohibited actions include sharing another person’s private information (doxing) or publishing contact details without consent. ## 12. Platform Integrity Users may not disrupt the platform, scrape user data, or pretend to be official representatives of VIBEKISS. ## 13. Automation and AI Misuse Users may not deploy bots or automated systems that simulate human interaction, including operating fake AI-generated accounts. ## 14. Security and Unauthorized Access Hacking attempts, reverse engineering platform software, or attempting to access confidential systems is strictly prohibited. ## 15. Offline Meeting Safety Users who choose to meet in person are encouraged to meet in public locations, inform trusted contacts of their plans, and arrange independent transportation. ## 16. Reporting and Blocking Users are encouraged to report violations including harassment, scams, or inappropriate content. Community safety relies on responsible user reporting. --- ## 29.
