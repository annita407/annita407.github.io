Community Guidelines

Last Updated: March 2026

⚠️ Safety Reminder

  • Protect your personal information.
  • Meet in public places.
  • Inform friends/family of your plans.
  • Arrange your own transportation.

VIBEKISS is a safe, respectful, and authentic environment. By using the platform, you agree to these standards.

1. Age Requirement

  • You must be 18+ years old to use VIBEKISS.
  • Underage accounts will be removed immediately without warning.

2. Respectful Conduct

Treat others with dignity. We strictly prohibit:

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation.
  • Abusive or degrading language.
  • Unwanted contact after being declined.

3. Hate Speech

Zero tolerance for content targeting individuals based on race, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality.

4. Sexual Content & Solicitation

  • No pornographic material or explicit imagery.
  • No requesting or offering sexual services.
  • Illegal content involving minors will be reported to NCMEC and law enforcement.

5. Scams & Fraud

Never send money to someone you meet on VIBEKISS. Prohibited: requesting gift cards, crypto, or investment solicitations.

6. Supplemental Terms

🇨🇦 Canada
Rights to access/delete data per PIPEDA and Quebec Law 25 apply.

🇦🇺 Australia
Compliance with eSafety Commissioner standards regarding cyber-bullying.

🇬🇧 United Kingdom
Duty of care to protect users under the Online Safety Act.

🇺🇸 United States
CCPA/CPRA rights for CA residents and NCMEC mandatory reporting.
