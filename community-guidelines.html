Community Guidelines

Last Updated: March 2026

⚠️ Safety Reminder

Your safety matters. While VIBEKISS provides reporting tools, moderation systems, and community guidelines designed to help create a safer environment, users must always exercise personal judgment and caution when interacting with others on the platform.

For your safety, please remember to:

  • Protect your personal information
  • Be cautious when sharing contact details
  • Meet new people in public places
  • Inform friends or family if you plan to meet someone in person
  • Arrange your own transportation when meeting someone
  • Report suspicious or inappropriate behavior through the app

If something feels wrong, trust your instincts and discontinue the interaction immediately.

Introduction

VIBEKISS is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and authentic environment where adults can connect and build meaningful relationships.

These Community Guidelines establish the standards of behavior expected from all users of the VIBEKISS platform.

By accessing or using VIBEKISS, you agree to comply with these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, content removal, feature restrictions, account suspension, or permanent account termination.

1. Age Requirement

VIBEKISS is strictly intended for adults.

Users must be at least 18 years old, or the age of legal majority in their jurisdiction (whichever is higher), to create an account or use the platform.

Accounts suspected of belonging to individuals under the age of 18 may be removed immediately.

Users who intentionally misrepresent their age may have their accounts suspended or permanently terminated.

VIBEKISS may report suspected violations involving minors to relevant authorities where required by law.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others with respect and dignity.

The following behavior is strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Threats or intimidation
  • Abusive or degrading language
  • Encouraging harmful or dangerous behavior
  • Repeated unwanted contact after someone declines interaction

Users must respect the boundaries and consent of others at all times.

3. Harassment and Abuse

Harassment, intimidation, or abusive behavior toward other users is prohibited.

Examples include:

  • Persistent unwanted messages
  • Insults or degrading comments
  • Threatening behavior
  • Attempts to intimidate or control other users
  • Emotional coercion or manipulation
  • Excessive messaging intended to harass

Users must not pressure others to share personal photos, contact details, or sensitive personal information.

4. Hate Speech and Discrimination

VIBEKISS maintains a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech and discrimination.

Users may not post, send, or promote content targeting individuals or groups based on:

  • Race or ethnicity
  • Religion
  • Nationality
  • Gender or gender identity
  • Sexual orientation
  • Disability

Content promoting extremist ideologies or hateful symbols may be removed.

5. Authentic Profiles

Users must represent themselves truthfully.

Prohibited actions include:

  • Creating fake profiles
  • Impersonating another individual
  • Using images of celebrities or other individuals without permission
  • Providing misleading identity information

Accounts determined to be deceptive or impersonating others may be removed without notice.

6. Profile Content Standards

Profile photos and user content must comply with community standards.

Prohibited content includes:

  • Graphic violence
  • Illegal activities
  • Hate symbols or extremist imagery
  • Misleading or deceptive imagery

Content violating these standards may be removed at the discretion of VIBEKISS.

7. Sexual Content and Solicitation

VIBEKISS is a dating platform but is not intended for explicit sexual content or sexual services.

Users may not:

  • Share pornographic material
  • Request or offer sexual services
  • Send explicit sexual images or videos
  • Pressure others to provide sexual content

Content involving minors in any sexual context will result in immediate account termination and may be reported to law enforcement authorities.

8. Manipulation and Coercive Behavior

Users may not manipulate, pressure, or exploit others.

Examples include:

  • Emotional manipulation
  • Fabricated emergencies
  • Threatening reputational harm
  • Exploiting vulnerable individuals
  • Encouraging unsafe behavior

9. Scams and Financial Exploitation

Users may not use the platform for financial exploitation.

Prohibited activities include:

  • Requesting money, gift cards, or cryptocurrency
  • Romance scams
  • Fake emergency financial requests
  • Investment solicitations

VIBEKISS will never request money through direct messages between users.

10. Spam and Promotional Activity

Users may not use the platform for spam or unauthorized promotion.

Examples include:

  • Mass messaging
  • Promotional links
  • Marketing campaigns
  • Collecting user data for external databases

11. Privacy and Personal Information

Users must respect the privacy of others.

Prohibited actions include:

  • Sharing another person’s private information
  • Publishing personal contact details without consent
  • Attempting to obtain sensitive information through deception

Users should avoid sharing sensitive information such as home addresses, financial information, or identity documents.

12. Platform Integrity

Users may not disrupt or misuse the platform.

Examples include:

  • Scraping user data
  • Misusing platform systems
  • Pretending to be official representatives of VIBEKISS

13. Automation and Artificial Intelligence Misuse

Users may not deploy bots or automated systems that simulate human interaction.

Prohibited actions include:

  • Operating fake AI-generated accounts
  • Manipulating engagement through automation
  • Conducting unauthorized experiments on users

14. Security and Unauthorized Access

Users may not interfere with platform security.

Prohibited actions include:

  • Hacking attempts
  • Reverse engineering platform software
  • Attempting to access confidential systems

15. Offline Meeting Safety

Users may choose to meet outside the platform.

For safety reasons, users are encouraged to:

  • Meet in public locations
  • Inform trusted contacts of their plans
  • Arrange independent transportation
  • Avoid sharing personal addresses early in conversations

VIBEKISS does not conduct criminal background checks and cannot guarantee the identity or intentions of users.

16. Reporting and Blocking

Users are encouraged to report violations including harassment, scams, fake profiles, or inappropriate content.

Users may also block other users to prevent further interaction.

Community safety relies on responsible user reporting.

17. Moderation and Enforcement

VIBEKISS reserves the right to investigate and take action when these guidelines are violated.

Actions may include:

  • Content removal
  • User warnings
  • Feature restrictions
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent account termination

Serious violations may result in immediate removal.

Moderation decisions may involve automated detection systems and human review.

18. Moderation Discretion

VIBEKISS retains sole discretion in determining whether user behavior violates these guidelines.

Moderation decisions may occur without prior notice.

VIBEKISS is not obligated to publish, maintain, or remove any content at a user's request.

19. Circumvention of Restrictions

Users may not attempt to bypass enforcement actions.

Examples include:

  • Creating new accounts after suspension
  • Using multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Attempting to manipulate platform systems

20. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for their own conduct and interactions with others on the platform.

VIBEKISS does not verify the accuracy of all user-provided information.

21. Platform Disclaimer

VIBEKISS provides a communication platform but cannot guarantee compatibility, conduct, or intentions of other users.

Users interact with others entirely at their own risk.

22. No Guarantee of Matches

VIBEKISS does not guarantee compatibility, successful matches, relationships, or outcomes resulting from user interactions.

23. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, VIBEKISS shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from interactions between users, whether those interactions occur on the platform or offline.

Users acknowledge that they assume full responsibility for their own decisions and interactions.

24. Platform Monitoring Disclaimer

VIBEKISS may monitor activity on the platform using automated systems and human moderation.

However, the platform is not obligated to monitor all user activity and cannot guarantee detection of every violation.

25. Service Availability

VIBEKISS does not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free operation of the platform.

The service may occasionally be unavailable due to maintenance, updates, technical issues, or circumstances beyond our control.

26. Updates to the Guidelines

These Community Guidelines may be updated periodically.

Continued use of the platform after updates indicates acceptance of the revised guidelines.

27. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

VIBEKISS may cooperate with law enforcement authorities where required by law or when necessary to investigate serious violations, including fraud, threats, exploitation, or activities involving minors.
