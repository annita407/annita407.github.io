# VIBEKISS COMMUNITY GUIDELINES ### ⚠️ Safety Reminder Your safety matters. While VIBEKISS provides reporting tools and moderation, users must always exercise personal judgment and caution. **For your safety:** * **Protect Info:** Keep your personal information private. * **Contact Details:** Be cautious when sharing phone numbers or socials. * **Public Places:** Always meet new people in public. * **Inform Others:** Tell friends or family if you plan to meet someone. * **Transportation:** Arrange your own way to and from meetings. * **Report:** Use the app to report suspicious or inappropriate behavior. > **Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, discontinue the interaction immediately.** --- ### Introduction VIBEKISS is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and authentic environment for adults. By using VIBEKISS, you agree to these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent termination. --- ### 1. Age Requirement * Users must be at least **18 years old** (or the age of legal majority in your jurisdiction). * Accounts suspected of belonging to minors will be removed immediately. * VIBEKISS may report violations involving minors to relevant authorities. ### 2. Respectful Conduct Treat others with dignity. Prohibited behavior includes: * Harassment, bullying, threats, or intimidation. * Abusive or degrading language. * Encouraging harmful or dangerous behavior. * Repeated unwanted contact after being declined. ### 3. Harassment and Abuse Intimidation and abusive behavior are strictly prohibited. This includes persistent unwanted messages, insults, emotional coercion, or pressuring others for sensitive personal information. ### 4. Hate Speech and Discrimination We have a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech targeting: * Race, ethnicity, or nationality. * Religion or disability. * Gender identity or sexual orientation. ### 5. Authentic Profiles Users must represent themselves truthfully. You may not: * Create fake profiles or impersonate others. * Use images of celebrities or others without permission. * Provide misleading identity information. ### 6. Profile Content Standards Content must comply with community standards. Prohibited content includes graphic violence, illegal activities, and hate symbols. ### 7. Sexual Content and Solicitation VIBEKISS is a dating platform, not a place for explicit sexual content. * **Prohibited:** Sharing pornographic material or offering sexual services. * **Minors:** Content involving minors results in immediate termination and law enforcement reporting. ### 8. Manipulation and Coercive Behavior Users may not exploit others through fabricated emergencies, threatening reputational harm, or emotional manipulation. ### 9. Scams and Financial Exploitation You may not use VIBEKISS for financial gain. This includes requesting money, gift cards, cryptocurrency, or promoting investment schemes. ### 10. Spam and Promotion Mass messaging, promotional links, and unauthorized marketing campaigns are prohibited. ### 11. Privacy Respect the privacy of others. Do not share another person’s private information (doxing) or publish contact details without express consent. ### 12. AI and Automation The use of bots, automated systems to simulate human interaction, or AI-generated fake accounts is strictly prohibited. --- ### 13.
