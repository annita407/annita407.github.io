VIBEKISS Child Safety Standards

VIBEKISS is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We actively monitor and moderate user content and behavior. Users can report any suspicious or inappropriate activity directly within the app.

All reports are reviewed and acted upon promptly. Accounts found violating our policies are permanently banned.

We cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required and comply with all applicable child safety laws.
