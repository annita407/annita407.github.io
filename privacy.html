By choosing to provide this information, you consent to its processing for the purpose of providing matching functionality and improving user experience.

Users may update or remove profile information at any time through their account settings.

Biometric Data for Age Verification

To comply with online safety regulations and prevent access by minors, VIBEKISS may utilize third-party age verification or age estimation technologies.

These technologies may process limited biometric indicators derived from a user-submitted selfie, such as facial geometry, solely for the purpose of estimating whether a user meets the minimum age requirement to access the Platform.

Biometric processing used for age assurance:

  • is used only for age verification purposes
  • is not used for facial recognition or identity tracking
  • is not used to train artificial intelligence systems
  • is processed temporarily and deleted after verification unless retention is required for legal or safety purposes

Age verification may be performed by trusted third-party providers who process this information on our behalf under contractual data protection obligations.

Users who decline to complete a requested age verification process may be denied access to the Platform or have their account suspended until eligibility is confirmed.
