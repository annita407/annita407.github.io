Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Welcome to Vibekiss. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you visit our application or website and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you in the United Kingdom and the United States.

Important Note on Consent: By using Vibekiss, you explicitly consent to the collection and use of your location and "Special Category" data (such as preferences and interests) to facilitate matches.

1. Information We Collect About You

We collect and process personal data in several ways:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, date of birth, and gender.
  • Contact Data: Email address and phone number.
  • Special Category Data: Sexual orientation or preferences (voluntarily shared for matching).
  • Technical Data: IP address, device ID, app version, and operating system.
  • Location Data: Precise Geolocation. We track your current location to show you nearby users. This is essential for the app's functionality.
  • Interactions: We store the content of your chats to ensure user safety and community standard enforcement.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK GDPR and US Privacy Laws, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a Contract: To provide the services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our app and ensure user safety.
  • Consent: Where you have checked a box or toggled a setting (e.g., Location Services).

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We only keep your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for. If you delete your account, we initiate a process to delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, unless we are required by law (e.g., investigations of harassment) to keep it longer.

4. United States Regional Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a resident of California or other US states with active privacy laws:

  • You have the Right to Know what personal information is being collected.
  • You have the Right to Opt-Out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your data (Vibekiss does not sell user data).
  • You have the Right to Non-Discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

5. United Kingdom Regional Rights (UK GDPR)

UK users have specific rights including:

  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure: Ask us to delete your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

6. Safety and Automated Decision Making

We use automated filters to detect spam, illicit content, and harassment. If our system flags your account, it may be suspended. You have the right to request a human review of any automated suspension by contacting support.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage and cookies to remember your login session and your preferences. These are "strictly necessary" for the app to function.
