Privacy Policy

VIBEKISS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the VIBEKISS mobile application and related services.

1. Age Restriction (18+ Only)

VIBEKISS is strictly intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect, use, or store personal data from anyone under the age of 18.

By creating an account and using VIBEKISS, you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years old
  • You understand that underage use is strictly prohibited
  • You acknowledge that any account found to belong to a minor will be permanently deleted

If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate the account and delete associated data.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

2.3 Payments, Subscriptions & Boosts

All payments, subscriptions, and boosts are processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store or have access to your credit card or payment details.

4. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

VIBEKISS uses Google Firebase services to operate securely, including:

  • Firebase Authentication

All data is stored on secure cloud servers with encryption, access controls, and security monitoring. Access is limited to authorized systems only.

5. Chats, Messages & User Content

Messages and shared content are stored to provide core chat functionality. Private messages are not actively monitored.

However, content may be reviewed if required for:

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.
